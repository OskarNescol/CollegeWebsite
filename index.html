Same game, new journey!

After story of Witcher 3, Ciri desides to embark on her own journey, follow her through multiple nations such as: Kaedwen or Redania.

Find new types of weapons and armors to make sure you are ready to battle those fierce monsters.

You will be able to venture our alone or with a companion, some of them you have met in Witcher 3 some are brand new ones. They will help you in combat, give you tips and have unique quests.

As a human you are unable to use signs and potions but instead you got more powerfull abilities from the "old blood"

Locations

You will travel many lands in the Northern Realms, some you have visited already in previous Witcher games but some have never been shown.

Travel in Temeria, Redania, Kaedwen, Skellige, Nilfgaard or Zerikania.

All of them are different types of terrain, from island filled with mountains to big and spacious dessert.

  • Skellige
  • Redania
  • Nilfgaard
  • Temeria
  • Kaedwen
  • Zerikania

Armors

There are still three armor types: Light, Medium and Heavy

From now on they will affect more than just your character weight, they will slow down your attacks and defend you against debuffs (bleed, poison etc.)

You will be able to change the looks of an armour to a different one you have unlocked before, keeping the original stats

You can paint the armors to suit it to your liking.

Weapons

From now on swords have differet types such as: long sword, daggers, curved blades

You will be able to use swords that are not common for witchers and are from far away lands

Revamped combat system allowing for more flexibility in your attacks, you will be able to cancel your attacks with a dodge.

You won't be able to repair swords infinite amount of times, everything must come to an end.
